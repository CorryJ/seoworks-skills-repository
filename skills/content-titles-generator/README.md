# Content Titles Generator

Generates catchy, magnetic article and content titles for any topic. Gives you control over writing style and tone before generating, so the output matches the intended voice of the content. Useful for beating writer's block, planning a content calendar, or quickly generating headline options to choose from.

Originally built as the Content Titles Generator Streamlit app by The SEO Works.

---

## What it produces

A numbered list of titles. Each title:

- Has a clear hook — a reason to keep reading
- Uses UK English spelling and grammar
- Varies in structure across the list (questions, how-tos, numbered lists, bold claims, benefit-led)
- Reflects the style and tone you selected

---

## How to trigger it in Claude

Just describe what you want. Examples that will trigger this skill:

> "Generate 10 content titles for the topic of AI & SEO"

> "Give me title ideas for a blog post about hiring in a recession"

> "I need a headline for an article on electric vehicle charging"

> "Suggest headlines for a piece on content marketing for B2B brands"

You do not need to say "Content Titles Generator" — any request for title ideas or headline suggestions will trigger it.

---

## Style and tone

The skill will always ask you to choose a style and tone before generating. You will be shown a short list of the most commonly useful options, and you can suggest your own if none fit.

**Available styles:** Academic, Analytical, Argumentative, Conversational, Creative, Critical, Descriptive, Epigrammatic, Epistolary, Expository, Informative, Instructive, Journalistic, Metaphorical, Narrative, Persuasive, Poetic, Satirical, Technical

**Available tones:** Authoritative, Caring, Casual, Cheerful, Coarse, Conservative, Conversational, Creative, Dry, Edgy, Enthusiastic, Formal, Frank, Friendly, Fun, Funny, Humorous, Informative, Irreverent, Journalistic, Matter of fact, Nostalgic, Objective, Passionate, Poetic, Playful, Professional, Provocative, Quirky, Respectful, Romantic, Sarcastic, Serious, Smart, Snarky, Sympathetic, Trendy, Trustworthy, Unapologetic, Upbeat, Witty

---

## Follow-up options

After generating, you can:

- **Refine** — change the style, tone, or angle and regenerate
- **Expand** — generate more titles on the same topic
- **Develop** — pick a favourite title and Claude will write an outline or intro paragraph for it
