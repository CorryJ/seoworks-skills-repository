# Persona Plotter

Generates 5 distinct audience personas for any topic. Each persona comes with 20 long-tail search phrases and a list of key questions, structured as a markdown table. Useful for content planning, SEO strategy, and audience research.

Originally built as the PersonaPlotter Streamlit app by The SEO Works.

---

## What it produces

A 3-column markdown table with exactly 5 rows:

| Persona | Search Phrases | Key Questions |
|---------|---------------|---------------|

- **Persona** — name, age range, occupation, and a one-sentence description
- **Search Phrases** — 20 long-tail phrases that persona would realistically type into Google, varied by intent (informational, commercial, comparison, navigational)
- **Key Questions** — the most important questions that persona would have, varied by type (practical, evaluative, comparative, trust-related)

---

## How to trigger it in Claude

Just describe what you want naturally. Examples that will trigger this skill:

> "Generate personas for an SME Marketing Manager looking to engage with an SEO agency"

> "Run persona plotter on solar panels"

> "Audience personas for sustainable fashion"

> "Who searches for electric vehicle charging?"

> "Map my audience for a B2B SaaS product"

You do not need to say "Persona Plotter" explicitly — any request to understand an audience or generate personas will trigger it.

---

## Follow-up options

After generating the table, you can:

- **Drill into a persona** — expand any single persona with more detail, additional search phrases, or a content brief
- **Reframe the topic** — regenerate with a different angle (B2B vs B2C, local vs national, beginner vs expert)
- **Export** — download the output as a Word document to share or present

---

## Example prompt from testing

> "Generate personas for an SME Marketing Manager looking to engage with an SEO agency"

This produced 5 personas: an in-house marketing manager at an e-commerce brand, a cautious marketing director at a B2B professional services firm, a junior digital marketing executive at a SaaS startup, a sceptical MD at a manufacturing SME, and a head of marketing at a multi-site retail brand looking to supplement an in-house SEO team.
